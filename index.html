About

We bridge the gap between computational intelligence and physical modularity.

Founded in 2014, Microarchi.tech focuses on high-performance spatial systems that adapt to the demands of modern urban environments. Our work leverages AI to optimize every phase of the architectural lifecycle, from initial generative design to long-term edge computing integration.

我們在運算智能與實體模組化之間搭起橋樑。

成立於 2014 年，Microarchi.tech 致力於打造適應現代城市環境需求的高效能空間系統。我們的技術運用人工智慧最佳化建築生命週期的每個階段，從初期的生成式設計到長期的邊緣運算整合。
